### Idea 1: Tulum Sanctuary - Organic Minimalism

**Design Movement**: Tulum Boho meets Japandi Minimalism - A fusion of Mexican coastal warmth with Japanese restraint

**Core Principles**:
1. Organic asymmetry - layouts flow like natural landscapes, not rigid grids
2. Earthy warmth - terracotta, sage, sand, and cream create a healing atmosphere
3. Textural depth - subtle linen textures, organic shapes, and natural materials
4. Intentional breathing room - generous whitespace that feels calming, not empty

**Color Philosophy**:
- Primary: Terracotta (#C4A484) - warmth, grounding, connection
- Secondary: Sage Green (#9CAF88) - growth, healing, renewal
- Accent: Warm Sand (#E8DED1) - calm, neutrality, peace
- Background: Cream (#FAF8F5) - clean, warm, inviting
- Text: Deep Charcoal (#3D3D3D) - readable, sophisticated

**Layout Paradigm**: 
- Asymmetric sections with organic dividers (wave shapes, curved edges)
- Off-center hero content with botanical accents
- Card layouts that breathe with generous padding
- Horizontal scrolling galleries for visual cards

**Signature Elements**:
1. Organic blob shapes as section dividers and decorative accents
2. Dried botanical illustrations (pampas grass, eucalyptus, palm fronds)
3. Woven texture patterns as subtle backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Gentle, slow transitions (400-600ms) that feel meditative
- Hover states that reveal depth through subtle shadows
- Swipe gestures for card navigation that feel tactile

**Animation**:
- Fade-up entrances with slight parallax
- Cards that lift gently on hover
- Progress indicators that fill like sand in an hourglass
- Smooth page transitions with opacity fades

**Typography System**:
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, editorial, warm
- Body: Lato (sans-serif) - clean, readable, friendly
- Hierarchy: Bold display titles, medium subheadings, regular body

## Selected Design: Tulum Sanctuary - Organic Minimalism

I'm selecting **Idea 1: Tulum Sanctuary** because it best captures the requested Tulum/Japandi boho aesthetic with:
- Warm, healing color palette perfect for relationship content
- Organic shapes that feel calming and non-clinical
- Strong typography pairing (Playfair + Lato) as specified
- Textural depth without being overwhelming
- Mobile-first swipe interactions for card galleries

### Implementation Notes:
- Use terracotta (#C4A484), sage (#9CAF88), sand (#E8DED1), cream (#FAF8F5)
- Playfair Display for all headings/titles
- Lato for body text
- Organic blob shapes as section dividers
- Subtle linen/paper texture backgrounds
- 400-600ms transitions for meditative feel
- Swipe-based card galleries on mobile
- Generous padding and breathing room
